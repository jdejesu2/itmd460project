VAMOS was comprised of professionals seeking to promote higher education to those who might otherwise be labeled as “lost causes”; to provide direction in short-term educational improvement and long-term college attendance. VAMOS was the next step to reaching and supporting a community who would have otherwise gone unnoticed.
