What is VAMOS?

Volunteering And Mentoring One Summer

VAMOS is a student-run organization at Illinois Institute of technology that exposes middle school minority students from neighbourhoods like the Back of the Yards to higher education and STEAM fields. This is done by providing free classes and field trips to help them discover and learn new things.

Our Mission

Our mission is to connect with students who are not the high achievers and help them reach their full potential in not only STEAM related fields and higher education, but by also embracing their Latino culture.
