What is VAMOS?

Volunteering And Mentoring One Summer

VAMOS is a student-run organization at Illinois Institute of Technology that exposes middle school minority students from neighbourhoods like the Back of the Yards to higher education and STEAM fields. This is done by providing free classes and field trips to help them discover and learn new things.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower Latino youth residing in the communities of Gage Park, Back of the Yards and Brighton Park to seek higher education and pursue careers in STEM through mentorship and summer immersion courses provided by current Latino university students. We connect with students who are not the high achievers and help them reach their full potential in not only STEAM related fields and higher education, but by also embracing their Latino culture.
