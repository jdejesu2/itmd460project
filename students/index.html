Become a Student!

One Summer

VAMOS is a student-run organization at Illinois Institute of technology that exposes middle school minority students from neighbourhoods like the Back of the Yards to higher education and STEAM fields. Applicants must commit to attending all program dates and meetings. Free breakfast and lunch will be provided to students as well as transportation. Only a certain amount of slots will be available this year! Student applications will be reviewed by VAMOS mentors. Preference will be given to students who have previously participated in VAMOS, Accepted students will be notified.

Who is eligible

  • Students whose grades range from D to B's.
  • Students who are 11 to 14 years old
  • Able to commit to ALL VAMOS classes three times a week
  • Students who reside in Back of the Yards, Gage Park, Brighton Park, or surrounding area.
