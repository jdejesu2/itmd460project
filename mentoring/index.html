Become a Mentor!

Mentoring One Summer

Mentors are students from colleges all over the Chicagoland area. Students are required to be enrolled in a 2-year or 4-year institute pursue a career in the STEAM field. Students will be placed in an internship with a partnered company. Students should also be eager and able to teach in almost any STEAM subject that is needed for the summer. Everyone with new and bright ideas is welcome to apply!

Timeline

  • April to May: weekly meetings for curriculum preparation
  • June to Mid-August: VAMOS 3-times a week classes

Requirements

  • Enrolled in a 2-year or 4-year institute
  • Above the age of 18 years old
  • Able to commit to weekly meetings two months before VAMOS
  • Good with children
